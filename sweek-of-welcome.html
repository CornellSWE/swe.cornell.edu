SWEek of Welcome

Welcome to Cornell Engineering and the Cornell SWE family! Below is a schedule of the 2021 SWEek of Welcome! Events are open to all Cornell students!

Thursday, 8/26
Welcome Picnic & Tie-Dye
Join prospective and current Society of Women Engineers members for a fun picnic lunch! Food will be provided by Wegmans and there will be several fun activities, like friendship bracelet making and tie-dye!

Friday, 8/27
Cookies, Cards, and Cornell
CCC combines networking building, professional development, and community service into one awesome event! Participants can decorate and (eat!) cookies, make cards for patients at Upstate Golisano Children's Hospital, and listen to our upperclassmen panelists talk about their experiences as female engineers.

Saturday, 8/28
Paint and Sip
Come show your artistic side with canvases, paint, music, and refreshments! Bring friends and come talk to members of SWE while getting to know your classmates. Participants will get to create their own artwork and take home their canvases and materials after.

Monday, 8/30
OSWE General Body Meeting
Come and meet the new members of our E-board!
