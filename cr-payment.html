SWE Corporate Relations (CR) Electronic Payment

Electronic Payment is available! Simply fill out the setup form and we will provide you with a secure link in your confirmation email. If you prefer to pay by check, please send checks to the following address:

146 Olin Hall
Cornell University
Ithaca NY 14853

Please select the option you chose for your session. For your reference, here are the options numbers and descriptions.

  • Option 1: No refreshments
  • Option 2: Cookies, soda
  • Option 3: Appetizers, soda
  • Option 4: Pizza, soda
  • Option 5: Subs, soda
  • Option 6: Pizza, cookies, soda
  • Option 7: Subs, cookies, soda
  • Option 8 : Appetizers, pizza, soda

Please note, prices are based on an attendance of 30 students. If your session requires food for a higher attendance, we will contact you with an additional invoice.
