ABOUT US
Who We Are
The Cornell Student Section of the Society of Women Engineers is open to all (male and female) undergraduate and graduate students in engineering and technical fields. Each year, we run numero"us programs and events to promote engineering, diversity, career and professional development, academic support, and pre-college education.
Our Mission
We promote women in engineering by advocating the importance of diversity and by uniting resources to

encourage academic, leadership, profession, and personal excellence

for the entire engineering community of today and tomorrow
