Corporate Relations Services

In addition to hosting your company information session, SWE Corporate Relations also provides the following services:

Publicity

Posters in campus buildings, e-mail invitations to student interviewees, listings on our SWE website, SWE newsletter, and a weekly College of Engineering newsletter.

Lounge Reservations

Phillips, Upson, Bard, Olin, or Hollister lounges all conveniently located in the Engineering Quad and near the Statler Hotel.

Audio-Visual Equipment

VCR, overhead projector, slide projector, and Dukane are available upon request.

Résumé CD

A compilation of résumés submitted by Cornell engineering students.

Cost

$300 base price per session. Refreshments are available for an additional fee.


If you have any special requests, we will make every effort to honor them.
