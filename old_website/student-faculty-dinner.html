Student-Faculty Dinner

The highly popular, semesterly Student-Faculty dinner is now in its sixth semester running and bigger than ever before! Come talk face-to-face with professors from all engineering disciplines in the largest student-faculty social run by students on Saturday, February 9th! Every semester, over fifteen professors from all engineering professions come to this highly anticipated event. This business casual dinner includes a chance to mingle with professors and their families, dine on catered food, and enjoy a cappella performances for only $5!
