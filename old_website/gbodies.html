General Body Meetings

Cornell SWE's General Body meetings are monthly opportunities for our members to gather to network and gain career advice from a variety of sources, from presentations given by company representatives to student panels. We provide a different social activity and food, as well as raffle prizes! Find out about all the awesome events we have coming up and take an afternoon to relax with your SWEsters! Fall 2021 and Spring 2022 G-Body Meetings will take place the first Monday of every month at 5:30pm. Check the SWEMail for locations!
