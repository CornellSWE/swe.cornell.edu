SWEek of Welcome

Welcome to Cornell Engineering and the Cornell SWE family! Every year, we hold a SWEek of welcome where students can mingle with other students and upperclassmen and learn about opportunities at Cornell! Check out our Google calendar and Instagram for the schedule!

Professional Netowrking Dinner

The Cornell University Society of Women Engineers proudly presents our semiannual Professional Networking Dinner in the Statler Ballroom. Held the evening before Cornell's technical career fair, the Professional Networking Dinner enables company representatives and students to interact in an intimate yet relaxed environment.

WE Conference

National SWE Conference is held in a different city every year and attended by over 15,000 attendees. It's an awesome opportunity to network with other female engineers, attend educational talks that range from imposter syndrome in the workplace to better decision making, and participate in the world's largest career fair for women engineers.

Women in Engineering Day

Every year we run Women in Engineering Day for accepted students, where we seek to expose admitted women to the opportunities at Cornell and the influence and strength of our section of SWE. We seek to foster a welcoming environment for our newly accepted students and celebrate their accomplishments.

General Body Meetings

Cornell SWE's General Body meetings are monthly opportunities for our members to gather to network and learn about upcoming events/opportunities. We provide a different social activity and food, as well as raffle prizes! Find out about all the awesome events we have coming up and take an afternoon to relax with your SWEsters!
