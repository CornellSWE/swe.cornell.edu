Communications

Student-Faculty Dinner

SWE Student-Faculty dinner is an opportunity for students to interact face-to-face with professors from all engineering disciplines in one of the largest student-faculty social events organized by students. With a focus on female faculty as a way to introduce more tangible role models to students, over ten professors from across the College of Engineering will come together with students and discuss academics, career paths, research, entrepreneurship, work-life balance, advice, what it is like to be a woman in STEM, and more, in an intimate and casual environment.

Outreach

Junior Girl Scout Day

Last semester, our committee planned a Junior Girl Scout Day in which the girls had the opportunity to earn their STEM Career Exploration Badge, as well as their Crane Design Badge. It was a great introduction into concepts that are used everyday by engineers. This semester, we are planning a similar event in which the girl scouts can earn their Investigator Cyber Security Badge and Balloon Car Design Challenge Badge. The event will take place in person on April 27th, from 6:00pm-8:00pm.

Brownie Girl Scouts

The Brownie Girl Scout committee plans one event each semester for scouts in grades 2-3 that lead them through interactive activities to earn a STEM related badge. In December, our virtual program went through the three Automotive Engineering badges: Design, Engineering, and Manufacturing. This semester, we are excited to transition back to in person programming and will be hosting our event on the Cornell Ithaca campus on April 30th. We will lead scouts in brainstorming, sketching, and building a plane to earn their Mechanical Engineering: Fling Flyer badge. Our goal is to introduce young girls to the world of STEM and inspire them to pursue activities relating to science and engineering in the future!
